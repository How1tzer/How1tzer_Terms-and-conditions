Privacy Policy for How1tzer Apps

Last updated: June 2026

Our applications, developed by How1tzer, are designed to work strictly locally. We highly respect your privacy, which is why we have designed our applications under the following principles:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, request, or store any personal information (such as names, emails, or passwords).
  • Local Storage: All information and data generated within the apps is stored exclusively in your device's internal storage.
  • No internet connection: Our applications work 100% offline and do not have permissions to send data outside your device.

By using any application developed by How1tzer, you agree that your data remains under your own control and on your device.
